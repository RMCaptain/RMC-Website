The Challenges

  • · Unauthorized 3P resellers undercutting MAP pricing across platforms, damaging brand equity and margin
  • · SEO indexing failures — products weren't ranking for core keywords, organic traffic nonexistent
  • · Single-channel ad strategy — only automatic Sponsored Products with no keyword, competitor, or brand targeting
  • · Broken catalog structure — Amazon mixed parentages, wrong sizing displayed, products difficult to find

What RMC Did

  • A/B tested titles and hero images — rebuilt listings for conversion rate, not just keyword density
  • Eliminated unauthorized sellers using Amazon enforcement tools, restoring full pricing control
  • Built multi-channel ad campaigns — Sponsored Products (keyword + competitor), Sponsored Brand Ads, defensive campaigns
  • Developed customer persona through data analysis — created enhanced A+ Content, infographics, and video
  • Rebuilt catalog end-to-end — correct parentage, accurate sizing, Subscribe & Save program launched
