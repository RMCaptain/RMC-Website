The Challenges

  • · Unauthorized 3P sellers obtaining and selling below MAP, undercutting margin and brand value across channels
  • · Over-reliance on a single SKU — new items weren't indexing and the broader catalog wasn't pulling its weight
  • · No advertising strategy — only auto Sponsored Products, no competitor targeting, no brand defense
  • · Fragmented catalog — products scattered across different parent ASINs, preventing cross-shopping and discovery

What RMC Did

  • Full content overhaul — carousel images, enhanced descriptions, SEO-optimized copy across all SKUs
  • Eliminated unauthorized sellers — restored MAP compliance and pricing control brand-wide
  • Full-funnel PPC — branded, category, competitor, and audience retargeting plus defensive brand campaigns
  • Consolidated catalog into variation families — enabled cross-selling, improved average order value
  • Subscribe & Save program launched with Vine seeding — grew subscriptions 15× in the period
